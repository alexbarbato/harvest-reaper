Privacy Policy

This policy discloses what information we gather about you when you visit reaper.isl.co. It describes how we use that information and how you can control it.

Information we gather and track

Harvest Reaper collects only the minimal amount of information necessary from Google to support authentication: name and account details. The information collected from Harvest and Google is only used to present data to consumers and not stored in any way on Harvest Reaper's systems.

We do not store any other sensitive information from Harvest or Google, to include:

  • Email addresses
  • Calendar events or details
  • Any Harvest information like Projects or Tasks

Personal information

We use the personal information you provide voluntarily to send information you’ve requested. We release personal information when we believe that release is appropriate to comply with the law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ISL, our users, or others. This may include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

Tracking

No tracking cookies or functions are used on Harvest Reaper.

Services

This site contains links to other sites. ISL is NOT responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web Sites.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All the data regarding users, their computers’ system information and benchmark results are stored on a server. This server is located in a locked and guarded space and only authorized personnel have access to the database containing given user names and passwords.

Notification of Changes

If we change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on reaper.isl.co. Users will be able to opt out of any new use of their personal information.

Choice/Opt-Out

Currently there is no data collected that is not 100% essential to the service and thus there is no choice or opt-out mechanism.

Reviewing personal data

You can review and validate the information, that we have collected about you by sending email to info@isl.co and requesting a review on your personal data.

Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web Site, you can contact info@isl.co or use the contact form at isl.co/contact.
